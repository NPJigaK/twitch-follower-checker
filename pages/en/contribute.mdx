Twitch Follower Checker is an open-source project. Anyone can easily contribute by reporting bugs, fixing issues, proposing new features, or improving documentation.

We warmly welcome beginners! If you notice any issues or have suggestions for improvements, please share your feedback by opening an [Issue](https://github.com/NPJigaK/twitch-follower-checker/issues).  
You are also encouraged to directly submit Pull Requests. Feel free to submit drafts even if your work is in progress.

Detailed contribution guidelines are currently under preparation. However, we actively welcome suggestions that enhance usability and stability (including backward compatibility).  
Your help in improving functionality and quality is highly appreciated.
