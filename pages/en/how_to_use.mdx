## Twitch Authentication

- On your first visit, click the `Authenticate with Twitch` button to perform Twitch authentication.  
- The displayed lists will reflect the followers of the authenticated user's channel.

## Checking Followers
### Follower List

Displays a complete list of users currently following your channel.

### New Followed List

Displays users who started following your channel after the last check date.  
The previous check date (reference date) is shown as `Last checked:` on the screen.

### Unfollowed List

Displays users who have unfollowed your channel since the last check date.  
The previous check date (reference date) is shown as `Last checked:` on the screen.

## Updating the Check Date

Clicking the `CHECK DONE` button updates the `Last checked` date (reference date).  
**Only click this button when you want to update the reference date.**

## Fetching Latest Information

Click the `REFRESH LISTS` button to update each list with the latest information.  
Use this feature when you need real-time updates, such as checking new followers during a stream.

## User Search

You can filter or search users using the search bar labeled `Search...`.  
You can search by date followed, username, or any other textual detail.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q. Can I manage multiple channels?
A. Only one channel per browser is supported. To check other channels, please use another browser.  
Additional functionality may be considered based on user requests made via [Contributions](../en/contribute/).

### Q. What should I do if loading is slow?
A. Fetching followers may take longer if you have many followers. Please wait without closing your browser.
